Camp Horizon ... changed my life forever. - Tara, 2006

Welcome to the electronic home of the Apocalyptic Squiddies!

The Apocalyptic Squiddies are a team that participates in the Calgary 24 Hour Relay every June. For more information about the relay check out our description of the event or head over to the official relay site.

We have built this site to provide information to our supporters regarding the relay and our team. We appreciate you taking the time to discover what we are about and the wonderful event we support each year. We hope you enjoy your visit. If you'd like to support us there are a number of ways to do so:

  • We're always pleased to receive donations. You can find more information here or sponsor us now.
  • Have a look at our fund raisers to get information regarding specific events leading up to the relay.
  • Start your own team! Head to the official relay site and click Register.

If you have any questions about the team or the site please contact our team captain, Dale. He's the one in the watermelon colored shirt below.
